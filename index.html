About Arrival In Greece

To Start off the trip my mom dropped me off in Chicago. The check in went smoothly; However, the flight did not. My flight got delayed about 4 hours and we sat on the plane for about three of those. I ended up missing my connection in Istanbul. When I arrived in Istanbul, I found my gate almost immediately, but I had about a 3 hour layover. So I went and explored the Airport. It was very nice and I have some pictures of it above. I then boarded the airplane and made it to Greece. I made it through to customs very quick and grabbed my bags to go get picked up. I managed to get a new Sim Card in the Airport as well.

A bus picked up the ACG students to head to the college. There was about 15 of us in our group. We all checked in and got our linens for our rooms. Then we grabbed our bags and checked out our rooms. We now quarantine until January 7th.

Freedom

I tested negative Friday Morning so I was able to get out of Quarantine. After being stuck in a room with 5 other guys I was anxious to get out and about. A big groupd from our building ended up walking around for about 5-6 hours during the late morning and afternoon. This was a good chance to be able to get out and view our area and find out how to get places and where things are.

While we were out, we stopped for lunch and I had a chicken crepe(above). After a little bit more walking we went back to our rooms to crash after walking for so long. After We all took naps we ended up meeting lots of people before we went out for the evening. I was able to meet a lot of people, and I think I ended up meeting everyone from my building throughout the day and the evening.

Week 1

This week was my first week of classes. A lot of my first few days were spent trying to figure out where my classes were and if I needed a textbook for it. Along with this figuring out how i would be able to access my meal plan provided enough stress for the week. A highlight this week was my roomate, Max, and I tried out for the soccer team on campus and made it. We are the only Americans on the team.

For the Weekend, I went shopping at H&M as Max and I needed coats because it is a little chillier than expected. This was fun to see the store and how it had more styles of clothes for cheaper than what we could get in America. On Saturday we made a trip downtown and got to see the Aacropolis and the surrounding area. Pictures do not do justice to how pretty it is. No matter what angel you look at the Parthenon or out at the city, it is breathtaking. It was amazing to behold and my favorite part of the trip so far.

Week 2 and 3

Sorry for the late upload. These last two weeks were very busy and chaotic. Last weekend was very fun. I was able to explore downtown Athens on Friday and find a park to watch a sunset. It was very amazing to see and such an amazing view along with seeings some historical sites as well. Saturday I went to the downtown and did some shopping in the modern square to explore some greek shops and the differences between american stores and the ones here in Greece. Then on Sunday I was able to make a trip to the Cape Sounio which was a place that I had wanted to visit and it was considered one of the most beautiful sunsets in Greece.

The week was then started off with one of the biggest snowstorms in Athens in the last 40 years. We got almost an entire foot of snow or more in one day. It drifted up for several feet. We lost power and the city was not prepared for the snow and the city lost power and was down for several days. Classes were cancelled for a few days. Along with this we couldn't get food for a few days and had to live off of sandwhiches and cereal. During this time, We built an enormous snowman named George. After this week, I spent the weekend exploring a few museusms to learn more about Greek Culture. I visited the Acropolis museum and the Cycladic Museum of Art both were very interesting. On Sunday, a group of us went to the beach and swam even though the water was very very cold. Then we were treated with my third beautiful sunset in two weekends. It was an amazing way to end the weekend.
