About Arrival In Greece

To Start off the trip my mom dropped me off in Chicago. The check in went smoothly; However, the flight did not. My flight got delayed about 4 hours and we sat on the plane for about three of those. I ended up missing my connection in Istanbul. When I arrived in Istanbul, I found my gate almost immediately, but I had about a 3 hour layover. So I went and explored the Airport. It was very nice and I have some pictures of it above. I then boarded the airplane and made it to Greece. I made it through to customs very quick and grabbed my bags to go get picked up. I managed to get a new Sim Card in the Airport as well.

A bus picked up the ACG students to head to the college. There was about 15 of us in our group. We all checked in and got our linens for our rooms. Then we grabbed our bags and checked out our rooms. We now quarantine until January 7th.
